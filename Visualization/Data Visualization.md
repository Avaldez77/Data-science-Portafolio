## ðŸ”¹ Exploratory Data Visualization
Exploratory data visualization is used as an **initial analytical tool** to understand data behavior.

Key goals include:
- Inspecting distributions and variability  
- Identifying trends, clusters, and outliers  
- Comparing groups, categories, or time periods  
- Guiding further analytical steps through visual evidence  

Visualizations are used iteratively to refine understanding of the data.
